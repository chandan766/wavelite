User Manual
1
Open the App

Navigate to the WaveLite homepage. No login or signup is required.

2
Enter Peer ID

Ask your friend to share their Peer ID or generate one for yourself. Input the Peer ID and click Connect to start listening for a connection.

3
Share Your Peer ID

Click Connect to create an offer, then share the connection key with your friend via any medium (email, chat).

4
Connection Established

Once both sides have entered each other's IDs correctly, a peer-to-peer connection is established automatically.

5
Start Chatting

After connection, you can start messaging. Text, images, videos, audio files, documents, and even your location can be shared instantly.

6
Use Gemini AI (Optional)

Click the AI button to switch to Gemini Assistant. You can ask it questions like a virtual chatbot. Save your API key in Settings to use this feature.

7
Theme & Settings

Customize your experience using the Settings modal. Manage your profile, STUN servers, and more.

8
Exit or Reload

Use the Refresh option in the menu to reset the connection and return to the start screen.
