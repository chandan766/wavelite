About

👨‍💻 Creator

WaveLite was created by Chandan Maurya in Bengaluru, India, in 2025. Driven by the lack of truly private messaging solutions that don't rely on centralized servers, Chandan set out to build a peer-to-peer communication tool where privacy, simplicity, and user freedom come first.

What began as a personal challenge quickly turned into a full-fledged app that empowers users to chat, share files, and even use AI — all without compromising their data.

🌐 About WaveLite

WaveLite Chat is a modern, secure, peer-to-peer chat app built on WebRTC. It allows users to communicate and share media directly without going through a central server.

✨ Features

  • End-to-End Secure & Serverless Communication
  • Send images, videos, audio, documents
  • Share live location with a map preview
  • Record voice and video from browser
  • Chat with integrated Gemini AI Assistant
  • Light/Dark Theme and Mobile-Friendly UI

⚙️ How It Works

WaveLite uses WebRTC to establish a peer-to-peer connection. The app exchanges offer/answer SDP using Cloudflare Pages Functions and connects two browsers directly. No account, no login, just fast, private chat.

🛡️ Security & Benefits

  • No central server: nothing is stored
  • Messages and files travel only between users
  • Zero configuration: works instantly
  • Open-source and customizable
  • Anonymous usage possible
